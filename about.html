About Us

Who are We?

We are a group of passionate intellects who have shown a high level of expertise in our different fields of interest. Careerexperience.NG was created to reduce the rate of unemployment in Nigeria by proving raduates with a platform to get the job experience required to scale through in their job search endaevours. We also provide an archive for employers to get capable and experienced personnels for job.

Our mission is to reduce the rate of unemployment in Nigeria by providing the youths with the experience needed to scale through the career..

Why Choose Us?

Over the years, recruiters charge an initial payment before offering a non-guarantee job opportunities to clients. But we are offering the internship opportunities without any upfront tuition.

Our Mission

TO THE GRADUATES

To provide the Nigerian graduates the opportunity to leverage their skills in peparation for the job they desire.

TO THE INDUSTRIES

To provide the industries with an archive of highly qualified candidates to choose from. Companies no need to spend so much training staff again.

TO THE GOVERNMENT

Our mission is to assist the government in the fight against employment and poverty within the shortest period of time.

TO THE SOCIETY

Providing employment for the youths will reduce the rate of terrorism/insurgency and also social insecurity which are caused by unemployment. This is bring about safert in our society.

Our Vision

We look forward to providing employment for 1000 graduates in the next one year. We also see ourself at toping the job recruitment table in future, hence becoming a globally trusted and reognized recruitment firm both by employers and graduates.
